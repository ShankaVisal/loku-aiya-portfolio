About me

I'm Damith Chamupathi and I'm a

Hi! I'm Damith Chamupathi Hewavitharana, a versatile professional in the realm of visual storytelling, with a passion for capturing moments that resonate. At 34, I've dedicated over five years to crafting compelling narratives as both a Camera Operator and Video Editor, having contributed my talents to esteemed ventures such as Vintage Film Production and Saman Wedding Planners.

In 2015, I embarked on an entrepreneurial journey, establishing my own sanctuary for creativity: a personal studio specializing in Photography and Videography. This endeavor has allowed me to cultivate a distinct artistic vision while seamlessly integrating my expertise in video and photo editing. My skill set extends beyond the lens, encompassing a deep understanding of social media dynamics and a solid grasp of information technology and computer hardware.

I thrive on the convergence of technology and artistry, leveraging my multifaceted background to deliver captivating visual experiences. Whether it's capturing the essence of a moment through my lens, meticulously crafting a narrative in the editing suite, or harnessing the power of digital platforms to amplify storytelling, I am dedicated to exceeding expectations and bringing visions to life.

Let's embark on a journey of creativity and collaboration, where every frame tells a story and every project is an opportunity to inspire. Welcome to my world of visual enchantment!
